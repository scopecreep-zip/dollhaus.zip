Our Priorities

The Doll Haus is committed to trans liberation - protecting trans women (especially Black & Latina dolls), guaranteeing gender-affirming healthcare, and ensuring safety and dignity for all trans people. We practice bimbo feminism: hyperfemininity as radical resistance, proving that glamour and intelligence aren't opposites, and that being politically fierce looks fabulous in pink. We support sex workers' rights, honor ballroom culture and chosen family structures, reject hustle culture for authentic joy, and build coalitions with all marginalized communities. We're pro-BLM, pro-choice, and unapologetically leftist - because being a doll means being politically powerful.
