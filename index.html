Our Priorities

President Doll is committed to: protecting trans women (especially Black & Latina dolls), supporting sex workers' rights, defending LGBTQ+ communities, dismantling patriarchy through hyperfeminine resistance, honoring ballroom culture and chosen family structures, celebrating intelligence alongside glamour, rejecting hustle culture for authentic joy, and building coalition with all marginalized communities. We're pro-BLM, pro-choice, and unapologetically leftist - because being a doll means being politically fierce.
