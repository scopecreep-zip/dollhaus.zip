# Governance & Policy Structure

## The Doll House Government Framework

The Doll House operates under a democratic governance system designed to ensure representation, fairness, and fabulousness for all dolls.

### Three Branches of Government

**Executive Branch**
- President Barbie - Chief Executive Officer of the United Dolls
- Vice President Ken - Second-in-command and advisor
- The Cabinet - Department heads overseeing key policy areas

**Legislative Branch**
- The Doll Congress - Bicameral legislature consisting of:
  - The House of Dolls - Representatives from all doll communities
  - The Doll Senate - Two senators from each major doll faction

**Judicial Branch**
- The Supreme Doll Court - Nine justices interpreting constitutional matters
- Regional Doll Courts - Handling local disputes and cases

### Core Policy Areas

**Education & Career Development**
- Universal access to Dream Academy programs
- Career training in over 200 professions
- Lifelong learning initiatives for all dolls

**Fashion & Creative Expression**
- Protecting the right to style and self-expression
- Supporting the fashion industry and designers
- Promoting sustainable fashion practices

**Health & Wellness**
- Comprehensive wellness programs
- Mental health support services
- Physical fitness and recreation facilities

**Environmental Sustainability**
- Plastic reduction and recycling initiatives
- Renewable energy transition for Dreamhouse power
- Protection of natural habitats and toy ecosystems

**International Doll Relations**
- Diplomatic partnerships with action figures worldwide
- Cultural exchange programs
- Trade agreements promoting fair toy commerce

### Legislative Process

1. **Proposal** - Any doll can propose new legislation
2. **Committee Review** - Specialized committees examine proposals
3. **Debate** - Full congressional debate on merits
4. **Vote** - Majority approval required in both houses
5. **Executive Review** - President Barbie signs or vetoes
6. **Implementation** - Agencies execute approved policies

### Rights & Responsibilities

**Every doll has the right to:**
- Pursue any dream or career path
- Express themselves through fashion and style
- Access education and healthcare
- Participate in democratic processes
- Be treated with dignity and respect

**Every doll has the responsibility to:**
- Respect the rights of other dolls
- Contribute to the community
- Follow established laws and regulations
- Promote positivity and empowerment
- Protect our shared environment

### Transparency & Accountability

The Doll House maintains transparency through:
- Public records of all legislative votes
- Open cabinet meetings (except matters of national security)
- Annual State of the Dollhouse address
- Citizen feedback and town hall meetings
- Independent oversight committees

### Amendment Process

The governance structure can be amended through:
1. Two-thirds approval in both houses of Congress
2. Ratification by three-fourths of doll communities
3. Presidential signature
4. Public notification and implementation period
