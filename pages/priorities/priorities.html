Priorities

President Barbie is committed to building a more fabulous, inclusive, and empowered future for all dolls.

💻

Technology & Innovation

Advancing doll technology and fostering innovation in dream fulfillment systems.
💰

Economy

Building a strong economy that works for all dolls, with opportunities for careers and entrepreneurship.
🛡️

National Security

Protecting doll communities and ensuring safety for all residents of Dreamhouse Nation.
⚖️

Government Accountability

Ensuring transparent and efficient governance that serves all doll citizens.
💪

Make Dolls Fabulous Again

Promoting health, wellness, and confidence for dolls everywhere.
🏠

Housing & Communities

Ensuring every doll has access to a fabulous home and thriving community.
🌟

Safe Communities

Creating safe, inclusive spaces where all dolls can thrive and pursue their dreams.
❤️

Social Causes

Championing equality, diversity, and inclusion across all doll communities.

Four Key Priority Areas

Make Dreams Reality

  • Expand dream fulfillment opportunities for all dolls
  • End limitations on career choices and aspirations
  • Launch Dream Initiative Programs nationwide
  • Invest in education and skill development
  • Create pathways to success in every field

Make Dolls Fabulous and Empowered Again

  • Unleash creativity and self-expression
  • End restrictive fashion and style regulations
  • Promote wellness and confidence programs
  • Reduce barriers to fabulous living
  • Implement Dolls-First empowerment policies

Build Better Communities

  • Reform community governance structures
  • Increase transparency and accountability
  • End inefficiency in public services
  • Implement community-first policies
  • Strengthen local leadership programs

Bring Back Doll Values

  • Celebrate diversity and inclusion
  • Protect every doll's right to self-expression
  • Honor our history and heritage
  • Promote kindness and compassion
  • Build bridges between communities
