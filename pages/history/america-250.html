America 250

Celebrating 250 Years of Dreams, Freedom, and Fabulous Achievements

1776 - 2026

A Quarter Millennium of Excellence

For 250 years, our nation has stood as a beacon of hope, innovation, and possibility. From the signing of the Declaration of Independence to the modern age of digital dreams, we've continuously evolved while staying true to our founding principles.

1776

The Declaration

The Founding Dolls declare independence, establishing the principle that all dolls are created equal and endowed with unalienable rights to life, liberty, and the pursuit of fabulousness.

2

1789

The Constitution

The Constitution is ratified, creating a framework for democratic governance that has endured for centuries. The first President takes office, beginning a tradition of peaceful transfer of power.

1863

Emancipation

A proclamation of freedom marks a turning point in our nation's journey toward equality and justice for all dolls, regardless of their origin or design.

1920

Votes for All

The expansion of voting rights ensures that every doll's voice can be heard in our democracy, strengthening the foundation of representative government.

1959

The First Doll

A revolutionary moment arrives as the first modern fashion doll debuts, changing the landscape of play, imagination, and possibility forever.

America 250

We celebrate a quarter millennium of democracy, innovation, and dreams realized. Together, we look forward to the next 250 years of making history.
