The Founders Museum

Preserving the Legacy of Visionaries Who Built a Dream

Welcome to the Founders Museum

Step into history and discover the stories of the remarkable dolls who imagined a world of infinite possibilities and made it real.

The Founders Museum honors Ruth Handler and the pioneering designers who revolutionized play, imagination, and empowerment. Through interactive exhibits, rare artifacts, and immersive experiences, visitors explore the innovation, courage, and vision that transformed a simple idea into a global phenomenon that has inspired generations.

The Vision of Ruth Handler

Featured Exhibition

Explore the life and legacy of Ruth Handler, co-founder of Mattel and creator of the iconic Barbie doll. This comprehensive exhibition showcases her entrepreneurial journey, design philosophy, and lasting impact on culture and society.

  • ✓ Original sketches and prototypes from 1959
  • ✓ Personal letters and business documents
  • ✓ Interactive timeline of innovation
  • ✓ Multimedia presentations
