Administration

The Mother

Executive Branch - Mother of the Haus

Leading through mentorship, protection, and community care - not domination. The Mother guides younger dolls, defends the most vulnerable, and prioritizes collective wellbeing over profit. Answerable to the house, not corporate donors. Redirecting military funding to community care and issuing protections for trans communities.

The House

Legislative Branch - Policy by Those Most Affected

Trans women, sex workers, BIPOC, and disabled folks lead policy creation. Operating on principles of representation, consensus, transparency, and community input. Legislative priorities: trans rights, sex worker decriminalization, healthcare as a human right, housing justice, reparations, climate justice, and prison abolition.

The Elders

Judicial Branch - Wisdom Through Lived Experience

Long-standing ballroom members, trans women who survived the AIDS crisis, former sex workers, civil rights activists, and disabled justice advocates. Practicing restorative justice over punishment, community accountability circles, harm reduction, and centering those most impacted by decisions.

Haus Council

Mothers & Fathers - Community Care Leadership

Operating on ballroom's haus system - mothers and fathers providing mutual aid, conflict resolution, political education, cultural preservation, and youth mentorship. Functions as alternative family with legal recognition, tax benefits, healthcare sharing, and inheritance rights. Leading with care, not hierarchy.
