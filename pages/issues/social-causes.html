Social Causes

Championing equality, diversity, LGBTQ+ rights, racial justice, women's empowerment, and disability rights for all dolls.

Overview

President Barbie believes that Dreamhouse Nation is strongest when every doll is free to be their authentic self. Our social justice agenda is comprehensive and unwavering: equal rights and opportunities for all dolls, regardless of background, identity, ability, or any other characteristic.

We're working to dismantle systemic barriers, challenge discrimination, and build a society where diversity is celebrated as a strength. From protecting LGBTQ+ rights to advancing racial justice, from empowering women in leadership to ensuring accessibility for dolls with disabilities, we're committed to creating true equality.

Through legislation, policy reform, education initiatives, and cultural change, we're building a Dreamhouse Nation that lives up to its promise of liberty and justice for all dolls.
