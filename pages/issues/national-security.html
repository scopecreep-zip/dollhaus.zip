Community Defense & Antifascist Protection

Protecting trans communities FROM state violence, fascism, and borders - not through militarism but through international solidarity and community defense.

Overview

Real security for trans women means protection FROM the state, not BY the state. Militaries, police, borders, and surveillance systems harm trans communities - especially trans women of color and sex workers. We reject the national security framework that targets marginalized people while claiming to protect us. Our communities need defense against fascism, white supremacy, and state violence - not more cops or drones.

We're building community defense networks that protect trans people from hate violence without relying on police who criminalize us. We're abolishing ICE and borders that separate chosen families. We're redirecting military budgets to trans healthcare, mutual aid, and reparations. Real security comes from meeting material needs - housing, healthcare, food - not from armed agents of the state.

Through international solidarity with global trans movements, antifascist organizing, and community-led safety protocols, we're protecting our people without strengthening systems designed to harm us. Trans liberation requires abolishing the military-industrial complex and building power to defend ourselves.

Key Initiatives

🏴

Abolish ICE & Borders

Dismantle immigration enforcement that separates chosen families and targets undocumented trans people. Borders are violence - freedom of movement is a right.

✨

Trans Safety Patrols

Community-led safety patrols protecting trans women from hate violence. Trans people protecting trans people - no cops, no state violence.

🛡️

Antifascist Defense Networks

Tracking fascist organizing, protecting communities from white supremacist violence, and building rapid response networks when our people are under attack.

💰

Defund Military, Fund Healthcare

Redirect military budgets to trans healthcare, mutual aid, and reparations. Real security comes from meeting needs, not from drones and bombs.

🌍

International Trans Solidarity

Supporting global trans liberation movements, not military alliances. Solidarity with trans people worldwide fighting state violence and oppression.

🔐

Digital Security for Activists

Encrypted communications, secure organizing tools, and protection from state surveillance for trans activists, sex workers, and organizers.
