Government Accountability

Ensuring transparent and efficient governance that serves all doll citizens with integrity and responsiveness.

Overview

The Doll Haus believes government must protect trans women first and serve all marginalized communities with radical transparency. We're building power structures that center those most vulnerable - trans dolls (especially Black & Latina), sex workers, and BIPOC folks - because when the most marginalized are safe, everyone is safer.

We practice bimbo feminism in governance: proving you can be glamorous AND politically fierce, intelligent AND hyperfeminine, demanding AND caring. Our accountability isn't about corporate efficiency - it's about community oversight, direct democracy, and ensuring those in power serve or step down.

Through community assemblies, participatory budgeting, mutual aid networks, and chosen family structures, we're building governance that protects the dolls while looking absolutely fabulous doing it.

Key Initiatives

✨

Trans-Centered Policy

Every policy decision starts with: "How does this protect trans women?" Centering the most marginalized ensures justice for all.

💅

Bimbo Feminism Framework

Governance that's glamorous AND radical - proving intelligence and hyperfemininity aren't opposites.

🏠

Chosen Family Recognition

Legal status for haus structures - because ballroom culture taught us how to govern through care, not control.

🤝

Community Assemblies

Direct democracy tools - participatory budgeting, immediate recall elections, and community veto power.

💰

Total Transparency

All meetings livestreamed, all budgets public, all votes recorded - because secrecy enables oppression.

💖

Mutual Aid Networks

Meeting community needs outside state control - food, housing, healthcare, childcare through solidarity not charity.
