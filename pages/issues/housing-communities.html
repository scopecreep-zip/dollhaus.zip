Housing & Communities

Ensuring every doll has access to a fabulous home and thriving community through affordable housing and neighborhood development.

Overview

President Barbie believes that every doll deserves a beautiful place to call home. Our housing initiative is focused on making dreamhouses accessible to all dolls, regardless of income level, while revitalizing neighborhoods and building strong, connected communities.

We're investing in affordable housing development, providing down payment assistance for first-time homebuyers, and implementing smart urban planning that creates walkable, sustainable communities with access to parks, schools, and essential services.

Through partnerships with local developers and community organizations, we're not just building houses - we're creating neighborhoods where dolls can thrive, connect with their neighbors, and build lasting memories.

Key Initiatives

🏠

Affordable Dreamhouses

Building and renovating affordable housing units to ensure every doll can afford a fabulous home.

🌇

Community Development

Investing in infrastructure, parks, and public spaces that bring communities together and improve quality of life.

🔨

Neighborhood Revitalization

Revitalizing struggling neighborhoods through strategic investment and community-led development projects.

💰

Homebuyer Assistance

Providing grants and low-interest loans to help first-time buyers achieve the dream of homeownership.

🌳

Sustainable Design

Incorporating eco-friendly design and green spaces into all new housing developments.

🤝

Community Building

Supporting programs that strengthen neighborhood bonds and create inclusive community spaces.
