Featured: The Founding Dolls

In 1776, a group of visionary dolls came together with a shared dream: to create a nation where all dolls could be free, equal, and pursue their dreams without limitation. These Founding Dolls risked everything to establish the principles of democracy, justice, and opportunity that continue to guide us today.

Through their courage, wisdom, and dedication, they drafted the Declaration of Independence and later the Constitution, establishing a framework for self-governance that has inspired dolls worldwide for generations.
