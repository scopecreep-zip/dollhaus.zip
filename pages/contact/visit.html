Visit The Haus

The Doll House is the People's House. For over two centuries, dolls from around the world have been able to visit and tour this iconic residence. We're delighted to welcome you!

How to Request a Tour

Public tour requests are scheduled through your Member of Congress. To submit a tour request, please contact your Representative or Senator's office.

Congressional Switchboard: 202-224-3121

Tour requests must be submitted at least 21 days in advance and no more than 90 days in advance. All requests are considered on a first-come, first-served basis.

Tour Hours

Tuesday - Thursday 7:30 AM - 11:30 AM
Friday - Saturday 7:30 AM - 1:30 PM
Sunday - Monday Closed

Tours are subject to last-minute cancellation. Please check your confirmation email before your visit.

What's Included on Your Tour

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I take photos during my tour?

Photography is permitted throughout the public rooms. However, flash photography, tripods, and selfie sticks are prohibited.

Are tours wheelchair accessible?

Yes! The Doll House is fully accessible. Please indicate any accessibility needs when submitting your tour request.

What items are prohibited?

For security reasons, prohibited items include bags exceeding 14" x 13" x 4", food and beverages, strollers, electric scooters, and other items. A complete list will be provided with your confirmation.

What should I wear?

Business casual or smart casual attire is recommended. Comfortable walking shoes are advised as you'll be standing and walking throughout the tour.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled tour time to allow for security screening. Late arrivals may not be accommodated.
