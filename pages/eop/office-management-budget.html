Office of Management and Budget

Ensuring every dream dollar is spent wisely to create maximum fabulousness for all dolls.

Mission Statement

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) serves as the President's principal advisor on budgetary, management, and regulatory matters. OMB's mission is to assist the President in developing and executing the federal budget, supervising the administration of the executive branch agencies, and overseeing the implementation of President Barbie's vision for a more fabulous nation.

We work to ensure that federal resources are allocated efficiently and effectively to support programs and policies that benefit all dolls. From dreamhouse construction to education initiatives, every dollar in the federal budget reflects our values and priorities.

OMB evaluates the effectiveness of agency programs, policies, and procedures, assesses competing funding demands, and sets funding priorities. We also oversee and coordinate the Administration's procurement, financial management, information technology, and regulatory policies.

Key Responsibilities

💵

Budget Preparation

Developing the President's annual budget proposal, ensuring resources align with priorities and advance the welfare of all dolls.

📈

Performance Management

Evaluating agency programs to ensure they deliver results efficiently and effectively for doll communities nationwide.

🛒

Federal Procurement

Overseeing government purchasing to ensure fair competition, value for taxpayers, and support for doll-owned businesses.

📋

Regulatory Review

Reviewing and coordinating all significant federal regulations to ensure they're effective, efficient, and equitable.

💻

Information Technology

Modernizing government IT systems to improve service delivery and protect sensitive doll data.

🎯

Strategic Planning

Setting government-wide management priorities and ensuring agencies implement best practices and accountability measures.

Leadership

Director of OMB

The Director of the Office of Management and Budget is appointed by the President and confirmed by the Doll Senate. The Director serves as a member of the President's Cabinet and leads a team of dedicated public servants committed to responsible stewardship of federal resources.

Working closely with agency leaders across government, the Director ensures that the President's budget priorities are implemented effectively and that taxpayer dollars are spent wisely to advance the common good.
