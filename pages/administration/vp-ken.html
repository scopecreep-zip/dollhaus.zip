Vice President of the United Dolls

Vice President Ken

A dedicated public servant committed to supporting the administration's vision and championing initiatives that strengthen communities across the nation.

About Vice President Ken

Vice President Ken Carson brings decades of experience in public service, community organizing, and economic development to the second-highest office in the land. His unwavering commitment to supporting President Barbie's vision while championing his own initiatives has made him an invaluable partner in governance.

Born and raised in Malibu, California, Ken developed a deep appreciation for community values and the importance of service from an early age. His parents, both educators, instilled in him a belief that leadership means lifting others up and ensuring everyone has a voice at the table.

Throughout his distinguished career, Vice President Ken has served in numerous capacities that have prepared him for this crucial role. From his time as a lifeguard—where he learned the importance of vigilance and quick action—to his tenure as a business executive and later as Governor, Ken has consistently demonstrated leadership, integrity, and a genuine care for the wellbeing of all dolls.

As Vice President, Ken has taken on significant responsibilities including chairing the National Infrastructure Council, leading the administration's mental health and wellness initiatives, and serving as a key liaison to state and local governments. His collaborative approach and ability to build consensus have been instrumental in advancing the administration's legislative agenda.

Role in the Administration

Vice President Ken serves as President Barbie's closest advisor and trusted partner in governance. He presides over the Senate, chairs key councils and task forces, and represents the administration both domestically and internationally. His portfolio includes leading initiatives on infrastructure modernization, mental health awareness, workforce development, and veteran support services.

Known for his ability to connect with dolls from all backgrounds, Ken has traveled extensively across the nation, listening to concerns, celebrating achievements, and ensuring that the administration remains connected to the everyday experiences of ordinary citizens. His "Ken Cares" listening tour has visited all 50 states, engaging directly with thousands of dolls about the issues that matter most to them.

Personal Life and Values

Vice President Ken is married to President Barbie, and their partnership both personal and professional has been central to the administration's success. Together, they have demonstrated that leadership can be collaborative, supportive, and grounded in mutual respect.

In his spare time, Ken is an avid surfer, fitness enthusiast, and advocate for outdoor recreation and environmental conservation. He believes strongly in work-life balance and has made promoting wellness and healthy lifestyles a cornerstone of his vice presidency.
