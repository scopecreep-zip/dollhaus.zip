About The Haus Council

The Haus Council serves as the executive leadership circle under The Mother (President Barbie), representing the most trans-inclusive, ballroom-rooted, and unapologetically femme governance in history. These exceptional leaders - majority trans women of color, sex workers, and ballroom legends - bring lived experience from the margins to guide our haus toward liberation, not reform.

Each Council member oversees liberation work in their domain: trans healthcare access, sex worker rights, mutual aid coordination, community defense, ballroom preservation, and abolition. Together, they form a powerful chosen family dedicated to protecting the dolls through bimbo feminist politics and antifascist organizing.

Under The Mother's leadership, the Haus Council operates through consensus and collective care - working alongside The House (legislative) and The Elders (judicial) to prove you can govern with glamour AND radical politics, intelligence AND hyperfemininity, fierceness AND compassion.
