Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

The Doll House is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website or interact with our digital services.

Information Collection

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your visit:

  • IP Address: Your computer's Internet Protocol address
  • Browser Information: Type and version of web browser
  • Operating System: Your device's operating system
  • Date and Time: When you accessed the site
  • Pages Visited: Which pages you viewed and how long you stayed
  • Referrer: The site you came from to reach us

Voluntarily Submitted Information

When you choose to provide us with information, we collect:

  • Email addresses (for newsletters and updates)
  • Names and contact information (when submitting forms or comments)
  • Messages and feedback you submit
  • Survey responses
  • Any other information you choose to provide

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Send newsletters and updates you've requested
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Enhance security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information for commercial purposes.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Government Agencies: When required for official government business
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Security: To protect the security and integrity of our systems
  • Service Providers: With contractors who help operate our website (subject to confidentiality agreements)

Data Retention

Information collected through this website is retained in accordance with:

The Presidential Records Act: Communications with the President, Vice President, or The Doll House may be preserved as part of the official presidential record.

Federal Records Act: Records are maintained according to approved retention schedules and National Archives requirements.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption

SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission

Firewalls

Network security to prevent unauthorized access

Access Controls

Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

Regular Monitoring

Continuous monitoring for security threats

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Session Cookies

We use temporary session cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies are deleted when you close your browser and do not collect personal information.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve content and functionality. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Websites and Social Media

Our website may contain links to external websites and social media platforms. When you leave our site:

  • You are subject to the privacy policies of those sites
  • The Doll House is not responsible for their content or practices
  • We encourage you to review their privacy policies

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy online. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Our website is designed for a general audience
  • If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it
  • Parents can contact us to review, delete, or stop collection of their child's information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from email communications at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us.
