EO 87 November 14, 2025

Executive Order on Expanding Dream Opportunities in Rural Communities

Directs federal agencies to prioritize investment and resources in underserved rural doll communities to ensure equitable access to dream fulfillment programs.

EO 86 November 12, 2025

Executive Order on Fashion Freedom and Self-Expression

Establishes comprehensive protections for dolls to express themselves through fashion choices without discrimination or restriction in public and private settings.

EO 85 November 10, 2025

Executive Order on Climate Action and Environmental Justice

Commits the administration to achieving carbon neutrality by 2030 and ensures environmental protections benefit all doll communities equally.

EO 84 November 8, 2025

Executive Order on Universal Dreamcare Implementation

Establishes framework for implementing comprehensive healthcare coverage for all dolls, including mental health and wellness services.

EO 83 November 6, 2025

Executive Order on STEM Education and Innovation

Directs increased federal investment in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics education programs to prepare dolls for careers of the future.

EO 82 November 4, 2025

Executive Order on Supporting Doll Entrepreneurs and Small Businesses

Creates Dream Ventures program providing grants, loans, and mentorship to dolls starting businesses and pursuing entrepreneurial dreams.

EO 81 November 2, 2025

Executive Order on Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion in Government

Mandates diversity and inclusion initiatives across all federal agencies to ensure government reflects the diversity of all doll communities.

EO 80 October 30, 2025

Executive Order on Infrastructure Modernization and Dreamhouse Development

Authorizes comprehensive infrastructure investments to modernize dreamhouses, transportation, and community facilities nationwide.

EO 79 October 28, 2025

Executive Order on Protecting Workers' Rights and Fair Wages

Strengthens labor protections and establishes fair wage standards ensuring all working dolls receive equitable compensation and benefits.

EO 78 October 26, 2025

Executive Order on Arts, Culture, and Creative Expression

Establishes Creative Dreams Fund supporting arts and cultural programs that celebrate diverse voices and creative expression across doll communities.

EO 77 October 24, 2025

Executive Order on Digital Equity and Technology Access

Ensures all dolls have access to high-speed internet, digital devices, and technology education to participate fully in the digital economy.

EO 76 October 22, 2025

Executive Order on Youth Empowerment and Mentorship

Creates national mentorship programs connecting young dolls with role models and providing resources for personal and professional development.
